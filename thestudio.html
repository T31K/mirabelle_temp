The Studio

About Mirabellé Decoupage

Located at Medan Putra Business Centre, the studio is tastefully decorated with decoupaged signboards, modern and minimum furnishing as well as beautifully completed works by students from all around the world who share the same passion in the beautiful art of decoupage.

Established since 2015, Mirabellé Decoupage has everything a decouper would need. Imported decoupage papers, paint, glue, varnish and all types of medium and tools that are widely used for decoupage are available at the studio.

Aside from decoupage, Mirabellé Decoupage has been a integral part of expanding decoupage influence on art & craft within the whole of South East Asia, as brand ambassadors of Fleur, COLORS, TO-DO, Decofoam, JAPA FUNE-MIYABI & Sospeso Trasparente.
